# Initial Concept
An ecommerce platform based on node.js microservice hosted in docker container and a frontend made with react. Read GEMINI.MD in docs folder for more details

# Product Definition

## Vision
A comprehensive ecommerce platform built with a modern microservices architecture to provide a secure, scalable, and reliable shopping experience. The platform serves both as a direct-to-consumer store and a marketplace for third-party vendors.

## Target Users
- **End Customers:** Individual shoppers looking for a seamless browsing and purchasing experience.
- **Store Administrators:** Business owners managing the overall platform, inventory, and global configurations.
- **Support Staff:** Dedicated users focused on customer satisfaction and order resolution.
- **Third-Party Vendors:** Sellers who can list their own products and manage their individual storefronts within the marketplace.

## Core Features & Goals
- **Core Shopping Flow:** Implementation of a robust product catalog, advanced search capabilities, a persistent shopping cart, and a secure multi-step checkout process.
- **Inventory & Product Management:** Sophisticated tools for managing diverse product types, stock levels, and categories across multiple vendors.
- **Order & Customer Management:** Centralized dashboards for tracking order lifecycles, managing customer profiles, and providing proactive support.
- **Marketplace Functionality:** Vendor onboarding, product listing approvals, and vendor-specific order management.

## Experience & Visual Identity
- **Professional & Trustworthy:** The UI will prioritize a clean, traditional e-commerce hierarchy that inspires confidence. High reliability, clear information display, and visible security features are paramount.

## Strategic Integrations
- **Observability:** Centralized logging, real-time metrics, and distributed tracing via the LGTM stack to ensure sub-second response times and 99.9% uptime.
- **Payments:** Seamless integration with Stripe and PayPal.
- **Logistics:** Real-time shipping calculations and label generation via FedEx, UPS, and local carriers.
- **Growth:** Integrated analytics (Google Analytics) and marketing automation (Mailchimp) to drive user retention and sales.
